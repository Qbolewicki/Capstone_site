In the U.S. a significant population is facing an uphill battle trying to thrive in an industry that has seen exponential growth in recent years. Women, who account for approximately 50.8% of the U.S. are statistically underpaid and underrepresented in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM).

Research generates hundreds of articles, infographics, and reports showing that women are capable of, yet not thriving in the field of technology.

U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics and PayScale have provided data from the past 6 years on top tech companies demographics and salaries. Using JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, this webpage was published as a tool to visualize and display data in the form of charts and infographics. The visualizations featured on this platform serve to raise awareness around the issue of gender discrimination in the field of technology and start a dialogue which address it.
